CAST

Anthony 'Tony' Stark

Robert Downey, Jr.

An industrialist, genius inventor, and consummate playboy, he is CEO of Stark Industries and a chief weapons manufacturer for the U.S. military. Wounded, captured and forced to build a weapon by his enemies, billionaire industrialist Tony Stark instead created an advanced suit of armor to save his life and escape captivity. Now with a new outlook on life, Tony uses his money and intelligence to make the world a safer, better place as Iron Man.

Virginia 'Pepper' Potts

Gwyneth Paltrow

A supporting character and romantic love interest appearing in books published by Marvel Comics, in particular those featuring Iron Man. Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Don Heck, she first appeared in Tales of Suspense #45 (September 1963).

James 'Rhodey' Rhodes

Don Cheadle

A friend of Stark's, and the liaison between Stark Industries and the United States Air Force in the department of acquisitions, specifically weapons development.

J.A.R.V.I.S.

Paul Bettany

Stands for Just A Rather Very Intelligent System, also stylized as JARVIS, or Jarvis, is a highly advanced computerized A.I. developed by Tony Stark, and was voiced by actor Paul Bettany, to manage almost everything, especially matters related to technology, in Tony's life.

Yinsen

Shaun Toub

Stark's fellow captive, who grafts an electromagnet to Stark's chest "to keep the shrapnel shell shards that wounded him from reaching his heart and killing him".

Harold 'Happy' Hogan

John Favreau

Hogan is hired by Tony Stark as his chauffeur and personal assistant after Happy saves Tony's life.

Obadiah Stane

Jeff Bridges

Stark's business second-in-command, mentor and friend who turns on him to overtake the company, eventually building a giant exosuit to fight Stark.

Justin Hammer

Sam Rockwell

Hammer is the reason why many of Iron Man's supervillain enemies have access to extremely advanced technology and why these foes use their equipment for violent crimes.

Natasha 'Black Widow' Romanoff

Scarlett Johansson

An undercover spy for counter-terrorism agency S.H.I.E.L.D. posing as Stark's new assistant.

Ivan 'Whiplash' Vanko

Mickey Rourke

A Russian physicist and ex-convict who builds his own arc reactor-based weapon to exact vengeance on the Stark family. The character is an amalgam of Whiplash and Crimson Dynamo.

Aldrich Killian

Guy Pearce

The creator of the Extremis virus and the founder and owner of the science and development organisation Advanced Idea Mechanics, who adopts the mantle of the Mandarin as his own. Killian develops Extremis to cure his own debilitating disability.

Maya Hansen

Rebecca Hall

A geneticist whose work helped Killian to create Extremis.

"The Mandarin" Trevor Slattery

Ben Kingsley

A British actor with substance-abuse problems whom Killian hired to portray the Mandarin, a terrorist persona in jammed television broadcasts, in which he is depicted as the leader of the international terrorist organization the Ten Rings.
